## Overall Learning 

The Generative AI course provided me with a strong foundation in understanding how modern AI systems work and how they can be applied to solve real-world business problems. I learned key concepts such as Large Language Models (LLMs), transformers, prompt engineering, embeddings, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). One of the most valuable learnings was understanding how AI models can process unstructured information and generate meaningful, context-aware responses. The course also helped me understand the importance of reducing hallucinations and improving trust through citation-backed and retrieval-based systems.

Beyond theory, the course gave me practical exposure to building end-to-end AI applications. Through projects like FinSight, I learned how to integrate AI models with tools such as FAISS, FastAPI, and Streamlit, and how to use Generative AI as a decision-support system rather than just a chatbot. Overall, this course significantly enhanced my technical, analytical, and problem-solving skills, while also helping me understand the growing role of AI in domains such as finance, consulting, and business decision-making.
